    KOVALAM OVERVIEW

    Kovalam Village, located in the Chengalpattu district of Tamil Nadu, is a vibrant coastal settlement covering 1.09 square kilometers. With a population of 10,887, it blends rich cultural heritage and modern development. The village offers essential services, including healthcare, with general, maternal, and emergency care; reliable water supply through overhead tanks and pipelines; and economic opportunities through fishing, agriculture, and tourism. Kovalam also benefits from government schemes like affordable housing (PMAY) and women’s empowerment programs. Known for its historical temples and scenic beaches, Kovalam is a thriving community dedicated to growth, sustainability, and well-being

    OVERVIEW OF KOVALAM VILLAGE

    Kovalam Village, situated in the Chengalpattu district of Tamil Nadu, is a dynamic coastal settlement that combines natural beauty, rich cultural heritage, and a growing infrastructure. Known for its scenic coastline, cultural landmarks, and tourism appeal, Kovalam is a village on the rise, serving as both a home and a popular destination for many. Spanning 1.09 square kilometers, Kovalam is a blend of old-world charm and modern-day progress, and it remains deeply committed to the welfare and prosperity of its people.

    With a population of 10,887, Kovalam is a closely-knit community where tradition meets contemporary development. The village administration has been proactive in implementing a wide array of government schemes and services aimed at improving the quality of life for its residents. These services cover essential areas such as healthcare, education, infrastructure, and social welfare, all aimed at fostering growth and sustainability.

    POPULATION AND DEMOGRAPHICS

    Kovalam’s population stands at 10,887 residents, consisting of 4,867 males and 6,020 females. The village is home to a diverse demographic that contributes to its vibrant cultural fabric. Kovalam's residents live across several distinct localities, each playing an essential role in the village's overall development.

    • Kovalam Main Area: The heart of the village, featuring both residential and commercial spaces.
    • Nehru Colony: A residential area with active community participation in local governance.
    • Ansari Nagar: Known for its religious and cultural diversity.
    • Semmancheri: A growing locality with increasing developmental initiatives.
    • Bajanai Kovil Street: A historically significant area, featuring a mix of traditional and modern elements.

    This distribution ensures that the community's needs are met across various areas, providing equal access to vital services.

    Kovalam is emerging as a vibrant tourism hub, attracting visitors with its pristine beaches, serene waters, and rich cultural heritage. Whether seeking relaxation or adventure, travelers find Kovalam to be a perfect destination.

    • Historical Temples: The Kalailasanaathar Kovil Temple, over 300 years old, stands as a significant spiritual and architectural landmark.
    • Religious Attractions: Ansari Tharka and Karnel Church draw both pilgrims and history enthusiasts.
    • Beaches & Natural Beauty: The village’s tranquil beaches and scenic landscapes offer a peaceful retreat for tourists.

    Kovalam’s growing tourism infrastructure, including hotels, resorts, and recreational services, supports the local economy while preserving the village’s traditional charm.

    WATER SUPPLY AND INFRASTRUCTURE

    Ensuring access to clean drinking water is a top priority in Kovalam, and the village has implemented a robust water supply infrastructure to serve the entire community.

    • Overhead Tanks (OHT): Kovalam is equipped with multiple OHTs, providing a combined storage capacity of 7,15,000 liters, ensuring a continuous supply of clean drinking water.
    • Water Pipelines: Over 275 pipelines span across various localities, delivering fresh and safe drinking water to homes and businesses with minimal disruptions.

    In addition to water supply, ongoing projects focus on enhancing road infrastructure, drainage systems, and street lighting to improve the village’s overall livability and accessibility.

    TRANSPORTATION AND CONNECTIVITY

      Kovalam is well-connected by bus routes, ensuring smooth transportation for residents and visitors. Regular buses operate between Kovalam and key destinations like Trivandrum, Nagercoil, and nearby towns, providing convenient and affordable travel options.

    • 🚌 MTC Bus 515A: Connects Kovalam to Thanbaram City.
    • 🚌 MTC Bus 109: Operates between Kovalam and Broadway.
    • 🚌 MTC Bus 109CT: Serves Kovalam and Thiruvanmiyur.
    • 🚌 MTC Bus 588: Links Kovalam with Thiruvanmiyur .

    SAFETY & EMERGENCY SERVICES

    24/7 Medical Facility

    Kovalam Panchayat ensures the well-being of its residents and visitors with a round-the-clock medical facility, offering emergency care, first aid, routine check-ups, and essential medications. Staffed by trained healthcare professionals, the center provides vital support, including maternal and child healthcare services. In case of critical emergencies, patients are quickly connected to nearby hospitals for advanced treatment.

    Lifeguard Services

    Kovalam’s lifeguard services ensure beach safety, with trained professionals stationed at key locations to monitor swimmers, surfers, and visitors. Equipped with rescue gear like life buoys and first aid kits, lifeguards are always ready for rapid response to emergencies. They also educate the public on water safety, preventing accidents and promoting a secure beach experience.

    CCTV Surveillance

    Kovalam prioritizes security with a comprehensive CCTV surveillance system covering public areas and key locations. These high-tech cameras enhance public safety, deter crime, and enable quick response to incidents. The surveillance network is actively monitored in coordination with law enforcement, ensuring a safe environment for all.
